# Phase 18 Public Playtest Share Copy

Status: ready

## Short Copy

Room Axioms is a browser deduction prototype where every rule is public, every investigation reveals one objective room fact, and the goal is to identify the hidden guests. This MVP playtest build has 12 cases and needs feedback on clarity, reasoning flow, controls, hints, and confusing moments.

Play here: `http://blog.onovich.com/RoomAxioms/`

## Medium Copy

Room Axioms is ready for public playtest feedback as an MVP release candidate. The build includes 12 checked cases, starts on `case-004`, and is intended to test whether the rules, board interactions, notes, hints, and final-answer flow feel understandable without showing hidden solver or target data.

Difficulty is not calibrated yet, so please treat difficulty labels as provisional. The most useful feedback is specific: which case you tried, where you hesitated, whether a hint helped, whether the final answer felt fair, and whether anything looked like it exposed hidden internal information.
