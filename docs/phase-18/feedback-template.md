## Handling Rules

- Record exact participant statements when available.
- Keep participant identities anonymous.
- Preserve P0/P1 reproduction notes before fixing.
- Do not convert internal metrics into playtest feedback.
- Keep difficulty uncalibrated until multiple real sessions are recorded and reviewed.
